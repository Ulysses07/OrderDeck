Live auction streaming has become one of the fastest-growing e-commerce channels of the last few years. But running an hour-long stream as a seller is harder than it looks. A typical seller has to:

- Track order messages from up to 4 different platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube)
- Capture username, product code and price for each one and pass it to a runner
- Reply to general comments, demonstrate items, keep the audience engaged
- Filter out spam, troll comments and links
- After the stream, manually verify the entire order list before fulfillment

Five lines, but in practice that's missed orders, wrong items shipped, and a tired seller.

## What OrderDeck does

OrderDeck collapses all that operational load into one Windows app:

1. **One panel:** chat from all connected platforms in a single chronological feed.
2. **One-click labels:** pick a message, type a price, label comes out of your thermal printer.
3. **Giveaway automation:** announce a keyword, OrderDeck collects participants, the wheel spins to pick a winner.
4. **Spam filter:** links, repeats and all-caps shouting are dropped before they hit your view.

## Local-first

Stream messages are processed on the user's own machine. They are never sent to OrderDeck servers, never stored. You open the app, connect, close the app, and they're gone.
